About Us


KOI is a website that explores the intercracies of Cryptocurrency. This website was designed quite unconventionally to traditional websites that look at crypto. This is because we show cryptocurrency through the eyes of an artists. We want to show how an artist would discover their way through the cryptoworld where they find out more about NFTs, bitcoin and ethereum. Discover more about how this website shows the journey of the artist through different blogs, data visualization and photography diaries.
