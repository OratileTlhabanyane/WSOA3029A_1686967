Close reading and reflection on Dimara, E. and Perin, C. (2020) ‘What is Interaction for Data Visualization?’

Posted Tuesday, 12 October 2021

Author: Oratile Tlhabanyane


This first describes what interaction is according to the field Huma-Computer Interaction is and extends to trying to describe interaction for visualization. However, it is said that interaction and visualization have no solid description that has been agreed upon due the broadness and wide term ‘interaction’. Dimara and Perin look defining interaction for data visualization in three steps: firstly, by identifying today’s view on “interaction from the visualization community, secondly revisit HCI view of interaction order to differentiate the two, and lastly using the two perspectives then propose a definition” (Dimara et al, 2020).


The first step of defining interaction for data visualization, which is to look at the current visualization view of interaction, they used different steps of research in order to receive statistics so that they can overall capture the view of interaction visualization. These steps of the research were done by emailing 77 visualization experts and collecting data from them during the period of 2 months.
