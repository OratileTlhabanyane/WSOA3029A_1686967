Critical reflection on the representation and communication strategies you used in your Data-Driven Artwork

Posted Thursday, 11 November 2021

Author: Oratile Tlhabanyane


I have never created a Data-Driven Artwork but i would want to achieve when creating is the ability to tell a story through coding and data. I would want to represent data, which accurate and 'perfect', through an imperfect lense. I have not really went about how i would create it but i ran into beautiful data-driven artwork during my research that i could look at for inspiritation.


I bumped data-driven art pieces on a website called Jfire where they use simple black and white animations that are driven by data. I liked how they showed simple and complex artpieces by just the use of two colors. The pieces were not interactive but the animation told the story of the individual pieces. I would like to implement the simplistic approach to my data-driven artwork, to show a 'deep', complex story through a simple use of two colours and simple visuals.
