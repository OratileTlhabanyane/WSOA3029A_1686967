StephenObi

StephenObi is a Nigeran photographer and videographer who was born in South Africa. He has worked with big brands such as Brutual Fruit, Smirnoff, Johnnie Walker and many more. He is based Johannesburg. One thing i appreciate about this man's work is how he tells a story with his images, from the angles that he shoots to the editing of the images is just incredible. His photography is like poem, the have rhythm that just speaks to the heart. Check out his website for many more of his work:https://www.stephenobiphoto.com/
